The best adventure of your lifetime

Join us as we travel to 4 different countries in a span of 7 days!

Countries Include

A vibrant, modern city-state, Singapore offers a blend of futuristic architecture, lush green spaces, and rich cultural heritage. Visit the iconic Marina Bay Sands, stroll through the Gardens by the Bay, and explore the bustling markets of Chinatown and Little India. Don't miss the delicious street food at hawker centers like Lau Pa Sat.

Malaysia boasts a diverse mix of cultures, landscapes, and experiences. In Kuala Lumpur, marvel at the Petronas Twin Towers and explore the vibrant street markets. Head to Penang for colonial architecture and incredible street food, or relax on the beautiful beaches of Langkawi. The lush Cameron Highlands offer cool respite and stunning tea plantations.

Thailand is a country of contrasts, from the bustling streets of Bangkok to the serene beaches of Phuket and Krabi. Explore ancient temples like Wat Pho and Wat Arun, indulge in world-renowned Thai cuisine, and enjoy the lively nightlife. Chiang Mai in the north offers mountainous landscapes, rich traditions, and a slower pace of life.

Vietnam is a land of stunning natural beauty and vibrant cities. In Hanoi, experience the charming Old Quarter and Hoan Kiem Lake. Cruise through the breathtaking Halong Bay, visit the ancient town of Hoi An, and discover the dynamic city of Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon). Don't miss the delicious pho and other Vietnamese delicacies.
